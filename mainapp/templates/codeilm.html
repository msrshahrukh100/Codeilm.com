Why Codeilm ?

Read the story of Russell to find out why you need Codeilm

After his adventures at the Paradise falls in South America, Russell is all set to learn programming and apply for programming jobs.
If someone asks him "what" all projects he did? He forwards them his LinkedIn profile.
If someone asks him about the source code of his projects, he gives them the GitHub repo url.

But then one day Mr. Fredricksen, Dug and Kevin came asking "how" he made his awesome projects and tell them the story of their projects.
Russell didn't know what to give them.

Then one day he discovered exactly what he needed.

So he created a "beautiful" story of his "awesome" project, and shared it with the world.
Everyone was very happy with him and his school gave him yet another badge for the "Best Programmer"
